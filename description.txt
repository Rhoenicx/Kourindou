Welcome to Kourindou Mod! 

This mod adds Touhou inspired plushies to terraria as collectable items, usually called Fumo's.
You're able to craft the plushies using our own unique crafting materials and crafting stations.

Besides the plushies we also aim to add a couple of other Touhou related items.

This mod is the spiritual successor of the old 1.3 FumoMod.
It is fully compatible with Gensokyo mod and even adds plushies as drops to their bosses and enemies.
